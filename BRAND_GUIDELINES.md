# WIND Shopping - Brand Guidelines 2026

The WIND Shopping brand represents a perfect blend of warmth and style—minimalist, premium, and professional. These guidelines ensure consistency across all customer touchpoints, content, and marketing materials.

---

## Brand Identity

### 🎯 Brand Spirit
- **Aesthetic**: Minimalist, premium, professional
- **Inspiration**: Zara, Massimo Dutti (contemporary, elegant, understated luxury)
- **Feeling**: Warm, sophisticated, approachable
- **Promise**: Elevated fashion that respects tradition and modesty

### 📝 Brand Name
**ALWAYS use "WIND Shopping"**
- ❌ Never: "brand Wind", "Wind", "WIND", "The Wind Shop"
- ✅ Always: "WIND Shopping" (full phrase, consistent capitalization)
- ✅ In emails/messaging: "WIND Shopping Team", "from WIND Shopping"
- ✅ In page titles: "WIND Shopping | Your Collection Name"
- ✅ In headers/footers: "© 2026 WIND Shopping. All rights reserved."

### 🎨 Visual Language
**White Space & Minimalism**
- Generous margins and breathing room
- Minimal visual clutter
- Let products and typography be the focus
- Use negative space to create elegance

**Typography**
- Primary font: **Cairo** (Arabic-first, Google Fonts)
- Fallback: System sans-serif
- Hierarchy:
  - **Display**: Large, bold titles (product names, section headers)
  - **Heading**: Medium weight for subsections
  - **Body**: Regular weight, ample line-height (1.6+)
  - **Captions**: Smaller, subtle for secondary info

**Color Palette**
- **Background**: Dark tones (dark navy, charcoal) - premium feel
- **Text**: White and light grays - high contrast, readable
- **Accents**: Warm neutrals (cream, soft gold) - warmth and luxury
- **Avoid**: Bright neons, oversaturated colors

**Micro-Interactions**
- Smooth hover effects (0.2-0.3s transitions)
- Subtle scale changes on interactive elements
- Smooth fade-ins for images and content
- Loading states should feel elegant, not jarring
- No jarring animations - aim for sophistication

## Content Standards

### ✨ Voice & Tone
- **Professional yet warm**: Expert but approachable
- **Respectful**: Honor tradition and modesty
- **Elegant**: Sophisticated language, avoid casual slang
- **Concise**: Say more with less; white space applies to words too

### 📦 Product Descriptions
**WHAT TO INCLUDE:**
- Fabric composition (material, origin if notable)
- Care instructions (washing, storage)
- Fit and style notes (silhouette, versatility)
- Design details (stitching, embellishments)
- Occasions (when/where to wear)
- Size guide and fit recommendations

**WHAT TO EXCLUDE:**
- ❌ Body figure descriptions ("hugs curves", "accentuates", "slims down")
- ❌ Sexualized or suggestive language
- ❌ Claims about personal characteristics ("makes you feel confident", "attracts attention")
- ✅ Instead: Focus on **elegance, flow, and comfort**

**EXAMPLES:**
```
✅ GOOD:
"Luxurious silk blend with fluid draping. Classic abaya silhouette 
with delicate hand-stitched details. Pair with our matching veil 
for a complete, elegant look."

❌ BAD:
"This flattering abaya enhances your figure and makes you look 
stunning. Feel confident and beautiful when you wear this!"
```

### 🕌 Halal Compliance
- **Modesty-first**: All designs respect Islamic fashion principles
- **No contradictions**: Never market "modest" and "sexy" in the same context
- **Content clarity**: Make modesty a selling point, not a limitation
- **Imagery**: Showcase garments on appropriate models; no revealing styling
- **Policy statement**: Include on policies page: "WIND Shopping is committed to Halal fashion—modest, elegant, and respectful."

### 👥 Review Moderation
**Approved Review Criteria:**
- Factual feedback on fit, quality, material
- Recommendations for occasions or styling
- Constructive suggestions for improvement

**Rejected Review Criteria:**
- ❌ Body figure references ("I lost 5 pounds wearing this")
- ❌ Suggestive language or comments
- ❌ References to non-Halal contexts or behaviors
- ❌ Inappropriate imagery or styling

### 📧 Email Communications
**Order Confirmations:**
- Opening: "Thank you for choosing WIND Shopping!"
- Include: Order number, items, total, estimated delivery
- Signature: "The WIND Shopping Team" or "— WIND Shopping"
- Style: Professional, warm, helpful tone

**Marketing Emails:**
- Subject lines: Elegant, not clickbait
- Content: Showcase design, elegance, modesty
- CTA: Clear but subtle ("Explore Collection", not "Don't Miss Out!")
- Unsubscribe link: Always present, easy to find

### 🏪 Homepage & Marketing Content
**Homepage Sections:**
- Hero banner: Showcase lifestyle elegance (not body-centric)
- Collections: Organized by occasion, style, season
- Featured products: Highlight design details, materials, versatility
- About section: Brand story, commitment to modesty and quality
- Trust signals: Secure checkout, quality materials, customer reviews

**What NOT to do:**
- ❌ Use words like "curves", "body", "figure", "sexy", "hot"
- ❌ Show styling that contradicts modesty (high splits, sheer overlays)
- ❌ Use before/after transformations or body-focused imagery
- ❌ Make claims about personal attributes gained from wearing

## Design Standards

### 🎨 Component Design
**Product Cards:**
- High-quality image (use Next.js Image for optimization)
- Product name (display font weight)
- Price (clear, no hidden fees)
- Quick size/color selection (subtle buttons)
- Hover state: Slight scale, soft shadow
- No busy background - let product shine

**Buttons & CTAs:**
- Minimum padding: 12px 24px
- Border-radius: Subtle (4-8px)
- Hover: Soft color shift, not jarring
- Label: Action-oriented ("Add to Cart", "View Details")

**Navigation:**
- Clean, minimal menu structure
- Clear hierarchy (main categories, sub-collections)
- Responsive on mobile (hamburger menu or slide-in)
- Brand name/logo at top

**Forms:**
- Label + input field (clear association)
- Placeholder text (subtle, supportive)
- Validation messages (helpful, not condescending)
- Submit button: Clear, prominent but elegant

### 📐 Spacing & Layout
- **Margins**: Generous (1rem+ between sections)
- **Padding**: Internal breathing room (16px+ inside containers)
- **Grid**: 2-3 columns on desktop, 1 on mobile
- **Aspect ratios**: Square (1:1) for product images
- **Line height**: 1.6+ for body text

### 🎬 Animations
- **Page transitions**: Subtle fade-in (200-300ms)
- **Hover effects**: Scale (1.02-1.05), not sudden jumps
- **Loading**: Elegant spinner or skeleton screens
- **Scroll**: Smooth scroll behavior (no jarring jumps)
- **Modals**: Fade background, slide or fade in content

## Imagery Standards

### 📸 Product Photography
- **Quality**: High-res, professional lighting
- **Styling**: Modest, on-brand (abaya on dress form or covered model)
- **Backgrounds**: Clean, minimal (white or dark backgrounds)
- **Angles**: Show design details (front, side, close-ups)
- **Avoid**: Revealing styling, body-centric angles, tight crops

### 🎞️ Lifestyle Images
- **Theme**: Elegance, confidence, tradition-respecting
- **Models**: Covered appropriately (hijab, modest styling)
- **Settings**: Sophisticated (home, modern spaces, outdoor elegance)
- **Tone**: Warm, relatable, aspirational
- **Avoid**: Beach/resort wear, body-focused shots, overly casual

### 📽️ Video Content
- Product demos: Show draping, movement, comfort
- Styling tips: Multiple occasions using the same piece
- Brand story: Values, craftsmanship, commitment to modesty
- Customer testimonials: Quality, fit, style (not body transformations)

## Questions & Clarifications

**Q: Can we say a design "flatters"?**  
A: Yes, but frame it as "elegantly drapes" or "flattering silhouette" without body references. Focus on design, not body transformation.

**Q: What about seasonal/trend marketing?**  
A: Include season/occasion context (winter abayas, modest formal wear) but maintain elegance. Avoid trend-chasing that compromises modesty.

**Q: How do we handle size inclusivity?**  
A: Celebrate extended sizing as part of elegance and respect. "Beautifully sized XS-3XL" not "All bodies welcome" (different implication).

**Q: Can we feature customer reviews mentioning body changes?**  
A: No. Ask customers for feedback on fit, quality, comfort, styling instead. Redirect body-focused comments.
